Tacos

Description

These tacos are filled with flavorful beef and topped with fresh ingredients for a perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain the fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water to the meat mixture. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  4. Fill taco shells or tortillas with beef mixture. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
