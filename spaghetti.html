Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a classic Italian dish that's easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked spaghetti with the sauce. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
