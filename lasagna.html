Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe that's perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain the fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce and diced tomatoes to the meat mixture. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Layer with lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Let cool before serving.
